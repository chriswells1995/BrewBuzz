About the BrewBuzz Team

The BrewBuzz team is comprised of three guys (and now you!) who love beer. BrewBuzz is an application that will let you search, locate, and review your local breweries. Once you have enjoyed your chosen establishment's delicious nectar, you can write a review and help spread the word. We regularly update our breweries so make sure to check in to keep up to date with what's brewing near you. Cheers!
